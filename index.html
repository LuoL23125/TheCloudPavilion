Our Services

Comprehensive wellness solutions for your health journey

Massage

Professional therapeutic massage to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote overall relaxation and wellness.

Tuina

Traditional Chinese therapeutic massage that uses rhythmic compression along energy channels to promote healing and restore balance.

Shiatsu

Japanese pressure point therapy that applies finger pressure to specific points to balance energy flow and promote healing.

Foot Reflexology

Specialized foot massage targeting reflex points that correspond to different body organs and systems for improved wellness.

Scraping (Gua Sha)

Traditional healing technique using smooth-edged tools to improve circulation, release muscle tension, and promote detoxification.

Cupping

Ancient therapy using suction cups to increase blood flow, reduce inflammation, and relieve muscle tension and pain.

Acupuncture

Traditional Chinese medicine technique using fine needles at specific points to restore energy balance and promote natural healing.

About The Cloud Pavilion Wellness Centre

Established in 2018 in Scarborough, The Cloud Pavilion Wellness Centre specializes in traditional therapeutic treatments and holistic wellness services. We are committed to helping our community achieve optimal health through time-honored healing practices.

Our skilled practitioners offer a comprehensive range of therapeutic services including Traditional Chinese Tuina Massage, Osteopathic Manual Therapy, Japanese Shiatsu Therapy, Foot Reflexology, Gua Sha (Scraping Therapy), Cupping Therapy, and Acupuncture. Each treatment is tailored to address your specific health concerns, whether you're seeking pain relief, stress reduction, improved circulation, or overall wellness maintenance.
