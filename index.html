What We Offer

Therapeutic Services

A curated selection of traditional and contemporary treatments — each designed to restore harmony between body and mind.

Massage Therapy

Professional therapeutic massage to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote deep relaxation and overall wellness.

Tuina

Traditional Chinese therapeutic massage using rhythmic compression along energy channels to promote healing and restore balance.

Shiatsu

Japanese pressure point therapy applying focused finger pressure to specific meridians to balance energy flow and stimulate healing.

Foot Reflexology

Targeted foot massage stimulating reflex points connected to body organs and systems, supporting whole-body wellness and vitality.

Gua Sha (Scraping)

Traditional healing technique using smooth-edged tools to improve circulation, release fascia tension, and promote natural detoxification.

Cupping Therapy

Time-honoured suction therapy that increases blood flow, reduces inflammation, and releases deep muscular tension and chronic pain.

Acupuncture

Classical Chinese medicine using ultra-fine needles at precise meridian points to restore energy balance and activate natural healing.

Personalized Consultation

Not sure what you need? Our practitioners will assess your concerns and craft a tailored treatment plan just for you.

Our Philosophy

A sanctuary built on tradition,
refined by care.

Established in 2018 in the heart of Scarborough, The Cloud Pavilion Wellness Centre brings together time-honoured Eastern healing traditions and modern clinical standards — under one calm, purpose-built roof.

Our practitioners specialize in Traditional Chinese Tuina, Osteopathic Manual Therapy, Japanese Shiatsu, Foot Reflexology, Gua Sha, Cupping, and Acupuncture. Every treatment is tailored to your body, your concerns, and your goals — whether you seek pain relief, stress reduction, or deeper wellness.

We don't treat symptoms in isolation. We restore balance, support the body's innate healing intelligence, and walk alongside you on a path to lasting wellbeing.
